OpenFlightGPS Android Permissions

Permissions

OpenFlightGPS contains no tracking software, no advertising software and no malware or adware of any kind. It does exactly what it says it is going to do - download maps (with your permission), and store those maps on your external storage, and optionally show your location on those maps that are georeferenced.

Your cell phone or network provider may keep track of the downloads that you perform, and they may charge you for bandwidth that you use. This is completely out of the control, interaction or knowledge of the maker of OpenFlightGPS.

OpenFlightGPS uses a minimal set of permissions in order to allow you to download and view maps, get GPS position and allow stopping screen timeout. Each required permission and why it is required is indicated and explained below.




Permission Reason
android.permission.INTERNET Needed to download maps
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Required to access secondary storage to keep the downloaded maps
android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Used to check the quality of internet access available (WIFI, 3G, etc)
android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Allow determination of your location for display on the map
android.permission.WAKE_LOCK Supports option to have screen on time out (turn off) during use
android.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE Find out if device has access to wifi for downloading maps
